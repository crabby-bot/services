What We Do

Our Tree Care Services

We provide a full range of tree surgery and garden services for homeowners and businesses across Leicestershire. Quotes available seven days a week.

🪓

Tree Felling

Complete removal of a tree to ground level, safely lowered in sections using ropes and harnesses where needed.

✂️

Crown Reduction

Reducing the overall size of the crown to lessen wind resistance and allow more light into your garden.

🌿

Crown Thinning

Selective removal of excess branches when a tree has become too thick, restoring light to plants below.

⚠️

Dead Branch Removal

Safe removal of dead and dangerous branches to protect people and property.

🌳

Shaping & Pruning

Careful shaping to improve the appearance and health of your trees and hedges.

🌱

Hedge Trimming

Regular trimming of leylandii and all other hedges. Essential for keeping fast-growing hedges under control.

🪨

Stump Grinding

We arrange stump grinding with a trusted local contractor, leaving your garden clear and tidy.

🧹

Garden & Site Clearance

Clearance of green vegetation prior to landscaping or building projects. All waste fully removed.

🪵

Logs, Woodchip & Mulch

We can supply logs, mulch and woodchip from cleared trees as requested.

About the Company

Meet Steve Maloy

Apples to Oaks Tree Care is a small, friendly business based in Glen Parva, Leicester — managed and run by Steve Maloy.

Steve started his working life in the motor trade before making the leap to the great outdoors and tree surgery. He attained his NPTC qualifications at Brooksby College, Melton Mowbray and is a proud member of the International Society of Arboriculture.

Steve personally attends every site visit to give advice and recommend work — always without obligation. Quotes are available seven days a week.

ISA Member International Society of Arboriculture — committed to the care and preservation of trees.
